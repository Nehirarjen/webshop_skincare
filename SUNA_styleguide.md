## Typography

### Headings (Playfair Display)

| Style | Size | Weight | Notes |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| H1 | Hero | Thin / Regular | Page hero |
| H2 | Hero | Thin / Italic | Italic variant |
| H3 | Section | 74px Regular | Section headers |
| H4 | 32px | Regular | Sub-sections |
| H5 | — | Regular | Small headings |

**Examples:**
- `H1` — Beauty in Simplicity
- `H2 (italic)` — *Radiant Skin*
- `H3` — Discover Your Glow
- `H4` — Natural Ingredients

### Body Text (Inter)

| Style | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|-------|------|--------|-------------|
| Body Large | Max | Regular | 160% |
| Body Medium | Medium | Regular | 160% |
| Body Small | Small | Regular | 160% |
| Nav / Label | Min | Medium | Uppercase, Letter Spacing 4% |

**Examples:**
> Experience the transformative power of Korean skincare with our curated collection of premium products.

> Our carefully selected ingredients work in harmony to nourish, protect, and enhance your natural beauty.

> Formulated with gentle, effective ingredients for all skin types. Dermatologically tested and cruelty-free.

> `SHOP NOW` — Nav / Text Medium · Uppercase · Letter Spacing 4%

## Abstände (Spacing)

### 8pt Spacing Scale

| Token | Value |
|-------|-------|
| 4xs | 4px |
| 3xs | 8px |
| 2xs | 12px |
| xs | 16px |
| sm | 24px |
| md | 32px |
| lg | 40px |
| xl | 48px |
| 2xl | 64px |
| 3xl | 80px |

### Section Spacing

| Token | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Container Padding | 24–48px |
| Column Gutter | 16–24px |
| Section Heading | 32–48px |
| Card Spacing | Size-dependent |

### Grid System

- **Columns:** 8 columns (Desktop) / 4 columns (Mobile)
- **Gutter:** Equal spacing between columns
- **Max Width:** Based on product grid
